🔍 Overview

Welcome to NurulBayt ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the website at nurulbayt.github.io and are committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit NurulBayt, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🕌 Our commitment: NurulBayt is an Islamic parenting resource. We handle all user data with the same trust and responsibility we bring to our content — with sincerity, care, and transparency.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate the website effectively:

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our newsletter voluntarily
  • Contact form data — name, email, and message if you contact us
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type (via Google Analytics, anonymised)
  • Cookie data — small files stored in your browser for functionality and analytics

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, health information, or government ID numbers.

💡 How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for these purposes:

  • Sending you newsletter emails if you have subscribed
  • Responding to your contact form messages
  • Analysing website traffic to improve our content
  • Displaying relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Improving website performance and user experience

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

🍪 Cookies

NurulBayt uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Functionality cookies: Remembering your dark/light mode preference
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

📢 Google AdSense

NurulBayt may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to NurulBayt and/or other websites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

🔗 Third Party Links

NurulBayt articles may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external website you visit through links on NurulBayt.

👶 Children's Privacy

NurulBayt is an Islamic parenting resource intended for adult Muslim parents and caregivers. While our content is about children, it is written for adults.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at noorwebsite01@gmail.com.

✅ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • The right to opt out of analytics and advertising cookies

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at noorwebsite01@gmail.com.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of NurulBayt after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
