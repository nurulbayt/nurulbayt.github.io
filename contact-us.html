Quick Answers

Common questions before reaching out.

How quickly do you reply?+
We aim to reply to all messages within 1–2 business days. During busy periods it may take slightly longer — please be patient with us.
Can I suggest an article topic?+
Absolutely! We love hearing what topics Muslim parents need most. Use the form above and select "Suggest an article topic".
Do you accept guest articles?+
We are selective but open to authentic, well-written Islamic parenting content. Reach out via the form with your idea first.
Is NurulBayt free to use?+
Yes — entirely and always. All articles and resources on NurulBayt are completely free for every Muslim family worldwide.
